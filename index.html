our history

We spent a decade with each other, and willing to spend rest of the decades of our life, with each other.

first meet each other

We first met each other in a tuition of higher secondary. We didn't talk with each other at that time, we just felt the existence of each other. We never thought that small eye contact would turn into something this big. Later, we met each other again on our first day of college... and the journey began!

proposal of marriage

After being with each other for a long time, I proposed her at a rooftop restaurant named Ozora. There was very loud music which we didn't like much, but we had each other. All sounds got faded over the sound of two hearts beating together.

proposal of love

After getting to know each other for some time, I felt that I want to spend rest of my life with her. I expressed my feelings and she replied "may be"! :P Later I was able to convince her to be a part of my life.

the engagement

This was more for our friends and families. We were engaged by our heart long before this. But as we say in India, marriage is not only between two souls, along with the souls, this creates a bond between two families. Our families celebrated our togetherness and blessed us with their presence.
